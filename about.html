About Me

My name is Travis Cashion. I was born and raised in the beautiful state of Colorado. I'm a devoted husband and obsessed with spending time in the wilderness, in that order. When seeking out experiences, I look to satisfy three conditions: excitement, adventure, and authenticity.


Thanks for visiting my blog, where I share thoughts and stories that are meaningful to me and have met these criteria. I hope you find something meaningful in them, too.
